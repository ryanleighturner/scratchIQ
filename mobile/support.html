About Palate

Palate is a beautiful and intuitive food journaling app that helps you document your culinary experiences, track your meals, and discover patterns in your eating habits. Whether you are exploring new restaurants, cooking at home, or maintaining a health journal, Palate makes it simple and enjoyable.

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I add a new food entry?
Simply tap the add button on the main screen, take a photo or select from your gallery, and add your notes about the meal.
Can I edit or delete entries?
Yes! Tap on any entry to view details, then use the edit or delete options to manage your entries.
Is my data private and secure?
Absolutely. All your food journal entries are stored locally on your device. We do not collect, share, or sell your personal data.
How do I delete my account and data?
Since all data is stored locally on your device, simply uninstalling the app will remove all your data. You can also clear all entries from within the app settings.
What iOS version is required?
Palate requires iOS 13.0 or later and is optimized for iPhone.
Does Palate work offline?
Yes! Palate works completely offline. All your entries are saved locally on your device.
Can I export my food journal?
This feature is currently in development and will be available in a future update.

Privacy & Data

Data Collection

Palate does not collect, transmit, or share any personal information. All food journal entries, photos, and notes are stored locally on your device only.

Data Deletion

To delete all your data:

  • Uninstall the Palate app from your device, or
  • Use the "Clear All Data" option in app settings
