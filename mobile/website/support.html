How do I get more scans?

You start with 25 free scans. Earn more by:

  • Sharing with friends (+10 scans per friend)
  • Posting on social media (+10 scans per post)
  • Using promo code "winning!" for 50 bonus scans
  • Posting winning tickets and tagging @ScratchIQ on TikTok or @scratchIQapp on Instagram

How accurate is the data?

All data comes directly from official state lottery websites and is updated daily. We calculate Real Time Odds based on current remaining prizes.

What does "Hot Tickets" mean?

Hot Tickets are games in the top 5% of Real Time Odds (RTO) nationally. These represent the best value tickets currently available across all states.

Can I buy tickets through the app?

No. ScratchIQ is an informational tool only. We do not sell lottery tickets or facilitate any gambling transactions. You must purchase tickets from authorized retailers.

Which states are supported?

We support 43 US states with scratch-off lotteries: AZ, AR, CA, CO, CT, DE, FL, GA, ID, IL, IN, IA, KS, KY, LA, ME, MD, MA, MI, MN, MS, MO, NE, NH, NJ, NM, NY, NC, ND, OH, OK, OR, PA, RI, SC, SD, TN, TX, VT, VA, WA, WV, WI

Why isn't my state showing any games?

Make sure you've selected the correct state in your Profile settings. If games still don't appear, try pulling down to refresh the list.

How does the camera scanning work?

Our AI analyzes photos of lottery ticket displays and identifies ticket names. Point your camera at a display wall, take a photo, and we'll match the tickets to our database.
